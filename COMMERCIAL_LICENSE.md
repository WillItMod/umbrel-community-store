A commercial license agreement can grant (as explicitly agreed in writing):
- Permission to distribute devices, images, packages, or releases containing

- Permission to use official WillItMod, AxeSuite, Axe app, or 5tratum branding
  and "official" tagging/marketing, subject to `TRADEMARK.md`.
- Optional commercial support or update terms (if offered).

## How to request a commercial license

- Open a GitHub issue in this repository describing what you want to do
  (countries, quantities, distribution model, and use-case).
- If there is an official email or contact listed in the repository, you may
  contact us there instead.
